# SAR Platform Survey - Google Forms Template

**Use this guide to create your survey in Google Forms for easy online responses and automatic data collection.**

---

## Quick Setup Instructions

1. Go to **forms.google.com**
2. Click **"Create" (+ icon)** → **"Blank form"**
3. Name: "SAR Platform Survey - Community Input"
4. Add description: "Gather feedback from SAR teams, agencies, and coordinators to prioritize features and modules"
5. Follow the sections below

## Data Collection & Export

### Best Practices:
1. **Collect responses here**: `https://docs.google.com/forms/d/YOUR_FORM_ID`
2. **View responses**: Google Forms → "Responses" tab shows:
   - Summary charts for multiple choice/checkbox
   - Individual responses
   - Download as .CSV for analysis

3. **Export responses**:
   - Click "Link to Sheets" to auto-populate spreadsheet
   - Data updates automatically as responses come in
   - Can create pivot tables and charts in Google Sheets

### Sharing the Form:
- **Share link**: Click "Send" button → copy link from "Link" tab
- **Email invitation**: Use "Email" tab to send to specific people
- **QR Code**: Generate QR code for posters/handouts
- **Embed**: Embed in website (useful for SAR organization sites)

---

## Tips for Better Responses

1. **Mobile-friendly**: Google Forms automatically optimizes for mobile
2. **Progress bar**: Helps respondents know how long it will take
3. **Conditional logic**: Use "Go to section" to skip irrelevant questions
4. **Required fields**: Mark truly critical questions only
5. **Validation**: For email/phone fields, add validation rules
6. **Incentive**: Mention they're helping shape actual software development
